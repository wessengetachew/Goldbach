Your Core Logic (Formal Text)

A reduced rational $r/m$ is in $\mathcal{C}_{\pi}$ if and only if:

1. **Angular Condition:** The angle $\theta(r/m)$ is congruent to $\pi$ radians ($180^\circ$).

2. **Class Condition:** $r/m$ is congruent to $1/2$ modulo 1.

3. **Reduced Fraction:** $r/m$ is exactly $1/2$.
